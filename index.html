Why we do this

One family. One destination. Seven trips back.

We didn't plan to become a South Africa travel company. We just kept going back — and eventually, our friends and family started asking us how.

Go South Africa exists because Cape Town is one of the most extraordinary places on earth, and most people who want to go don't know anyone who can show it to them properly. We can. We have. We'd like to take you.
