Our story

We went once. Then we couldn't stop.

Go South Africa is a family business — in the most literal sense. One family, one destination, built over fifteen years of going back.

It started with a single trip. We went to Cape Town the way most people do — curious, slightly nervous, not sure what to expect. By Day 3, we understood why people who've been keep telling everyone they know to go.

So we went back. Then again. We took our parents. We dragged a husband along. We went seven times before anyone said out loud that we should probably just start a company.

Go South Africa exists because Cape Town is one of the most extraordinary places on earth — and because the people who want to visit most are often the ones with the least guidance on how to do it. The city is enormous, the options are overwhelming, and the planning can stop you before you start.

We've done the planning. We know which Table Mountain queue to skip. We know where to sit at The Lookout in Hout Bay. We know which Constantia wine estate deserves a full afternoon and which one is worth a single glass. We know that the Aquila safari departs at 7:00 am and that an early night the day before is worth it.

Between our team, we've visited over 70 countries. We keep coming back to one. That's the whole pitch.

"Cape Town isn't just a great trip. It's the trip you tell people about for the rest of your life."
